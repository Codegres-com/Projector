# Team Management

The Team Management module facilitates the administration of agency staff and resources. It allows for the onboarding, organization, and management of team members, ensuring that the right people with the correct roles and permissions are available for assignment to projects and tasks.

## Features

| Feature Name | Description | Priority | User Benefit |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Add Team Member** | Functionality to invite or create a new team member profile, including basic information (Name, Email) and initial role assignment. | **High** | Enables the agency to onboard new staff and prepare them for project assignments. |
| **Edit Team Member** | Tools to update team member details, such as contact information, skills, and availability. | **Medium** | Ensures that team member records remain accurate and up-to-date. |
| **View Team Member** | A detailed view of a team member's profile, including their role, assigned projects, and contact details. | **High** | Provides quick access to team member information for resource planning. |
| **Delete Team Member** | Ability to remove or deactivate a team member's account from the system. | **Low** | Allows for the secure offboarding of staff who have left the agency. |
| **Role Assignment** | Logic to assign or modify the specific role of a team member (e.g., Admin, Project Manager, Developer), determining their access and permissions. | **High** | Ensures proper security and access control within the platform. |
| **Team Directory** | A searchable list or grid view of all team members. | **Medium** | Helps management quickly find and organize staff. |
